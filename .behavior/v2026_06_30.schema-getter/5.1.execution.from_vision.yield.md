## deviations from vision

- **none material.** the vision's open questions were all resolved before execution.
- **one type-design detail**: `getContract`'s param is typed *structurally* (the statics it
  reads), not as `typeof DomainObject` — because `typeof DomainObject`'s generic `<T>`
  construct signature is not satisfied by concrete subclass constructors (`typeof Job`).
  this keeps both `getContract(Job)` (direct) and `DomainObject.contract` (`this`) callable.
